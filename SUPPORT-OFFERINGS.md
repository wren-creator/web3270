# WebTerm/3270 — Support & Services Offerings

WebTerm/3270 is used by organizations to provide browser-based access to production z/OS and z/VM systems, and increasingly for security education against live mainframe environments. When a tool sits between your team and production LPARs, support responsiveness matters as much as the software itself. These plans exist for organizations that need a committed point of contact, a defined response window, and direct assistance when something goes wrong — rather than relying on best-effort community support.

All tiers are annual subscriptions and include full access to WebTerm/3270 releases and security updates.

## Service-Level Agreement (Response Times)

SLAs below are **response time commitments**, not resolution guarantees — how quickly you'll hear back and engagement begins, not a promise of a fix by a specific deadline. Given the nature of protocol-level and mainframe-adjacent issues, actual fix timelines vary by root cause.

| Severity | Definition | Priority ($5k) | Direct ($10k) | On-Site ($20k) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Critical** | Bridge/service down, cannot connect to any LPAR, security branch credential exposure | 1 business day | 4 business hours | 2 business hours |
| **High** | Major feature broken (file transfer, macro engine, multi-session tabs, security toolkit) | 2 business days | 1 business day | 4 business hours |
| **Normal** | Minor bugs, cosmetic issues, general questions, feature requests | 3 business days | 2 business days | 1 business day |

Support hours: Monday–Friday, standard business hours (time zone confirmed at contract signing). Off-hours emergency response is not included at any tier and can be discussed as a custom add-on.

## What's Not Included

- New feature development outside the existing roadmap (available as separate paid custom development)
- Support for forks or heavily modified deployments diverging from the published codebase
- On-call/24-7 coverage or after-hours emergency response
- Migration of unrelated legacy TN3270 tooling to WebTerm/3270
- Training sessions beyond what's covered in an on-site engagement (available separately)

## Tier Details

### Priority Support — $5,000/year
- Priority email support for bugs and issues
- Priority placement on new feature/enhancement requests
- One-time custom branding: your company colors and logo applied to the terminal UI

### Direct Support — $10,000/year
Everything in Priority Support, plus:
- Live support via FaceTime, phone, and email
- Time-boxed remote access session (VPN or screen-share) for diagnostics and fix assistance, scheduled per incident — not standing/open access

### On-Site Support — $20,000/year + travel expenses
Everything in Direct Support, plus:
- Up to one week of on-site support per year
- Travel expenses (airfare, lodging, etc.) billed separately, at cost
- Additional on-site trips beyond the included week available at a discounted day rate

## Contract Terms

- **Billing:** Annual, invoiced at contract start. Net 30 payment terms.
- **Renewal:** Auto-renews annually unless cancelled with 30 days' written notice prior to the renewal date.
- **Cancellation:** No refunds for partial-year cancellation; support continues through the paid term.
- **Scope changes:** Upgrading tiers mid-term is prorated for the remainder of the contract year; downgrades take effect at renewal.

---

## Confidentiality

Support engagements may involve exposure to your production system configuration, screen content, or credentials during live troubleshooting. All support is provided under mutual confidentiality; a signed NDA is available on request and required for on-site or VPN-based engagements involving production LPARs.
